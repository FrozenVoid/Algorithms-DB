Content is rated by total
unique users which viewed it,
divided by total unique users
on website/category in specific period.

Formula: Viewers/(1+Total)
Example: there are 500 users
online in last hour, but only
20 viewed content_A, making its
'rating' equal to 20/501=0.0399
vs 200 viewed content B
which is rated 200/501=0.399
