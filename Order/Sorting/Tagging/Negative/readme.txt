Negative tags are votes against a specific
tag. They are used in system with
limited tag votes per content piece(e.g.
20 votes +tag/-tag)
Example: 
3 people tagged X +anime
2 people tagged X -anime
the tagscore is +1
and filters will show:
anime:2 -> shown as at least two voted for
anime:+2 ->not shown as score is +1
anime:-1 ->not show as score is above
